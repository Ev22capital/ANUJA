About Us

Anuja Agarwal
Founder, EV22 Capital

Anuja Agarwal has spent two decades inside global markets. An IIM Ahmedabad MBA, she began at Bank of America and made markets as a sell-side derivatives prop trader across vanilla and exotic products. Since 2016 she has held senior buyside roles — most recently as a long-short specialist with a strong investment track record across cycles. Her work as Head of Research for India and Japan at ACGA (Asian Corporate Governance Association) has sharpened her investment conviction with governance advocacy.

EV22 Capital backs the companies, and the policy, that carry markets into the 22nd century.
